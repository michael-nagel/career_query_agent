I am an economist, data scientist, and AI/LLM engineer currently working as a Consultant for AI and Data Analytics at P3 Group in Germany. My work focuses on bridging the gap between complex statistical theory and practical industrial application.

I recently completed my PhD at the University of Tübingen, where my research centered on quantifying behavioral responses in economics using probabilistic methods and advanced regularization for high-dimensional statistical models. During my time in academia, I also taught multivariate methods and structural equation modeling while managing administrative tasks like applicant interviews for Master's programs.

Beyond my research, I have gained valuable industry experience at Mercedes-Benz AG, where I served as an HR Manager coordinating workforce resources and as a Planning Coordinator for production tests. This combination of deep technical expertise and corporate experience allows me to design data-driven solutions that are both statistically rigorous and aligned with business requirements.
